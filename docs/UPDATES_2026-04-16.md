# ScholarPath Graduation Progress — Updates (April 16, 2026)

## New Features

### Credit Progress Timeline
A new visual chart on every student's dashboard showing cumulative credits earned over time compared to the expected graduation pace. Students who fall behind and catch up will see their progress line climb — a motivational tool for at-risk students.

- **Visible to:** Students (on their dashboard) and Counselors (on the student Progress tab)
- **How it works:** Credits are plotted by trimester, with a dashed "expected pace" line and a badge showing "On Pace" or "Behind Pace"

### All Students View for Advisors
Counselors and case managers can now toggle between **"My Students"** and **"All Students"** on their dashboard. This allows advisors to view and work with any student in the school — useful for next-year forecasting and cross-caseload support.

- **How to use:** Look for the toggle at the top of your student list. Default view remains your assigned caseload.

## Data Fixes

### MTSS Intervention Tracker
The MTSS tab was showing no data due to a missing school identifier on student notes. All 1,732 existing notes have been corrected, and new notes will be tagged correctly going forward. The MTSS tracker now displays all intervention data as expected.

### Contact Snapshot
The Contact tab was missing note counts from staff members who are not in a counselor role. This has been corrected — all staff who log notes (including case managers, viewers, and registrars) now appear in the contact roll-up.
